The Amazing Spider-Man Overview

A teenage Peter Parker grapples with both high school and amazing super-human crises as his alter-ego Spider-Man. Peter Parker, an outcast high school student, gets bitten by a radioactive spider and attains superpowers. Soon, he is forced to use his abilities to fight a monstrous foe. After Peter Parker is bitten by a genetically altered spider, he gains newfound, spider-like powers and ventures out to save the city from the machinations of a mysterious reptilian foe.
